Reservations are always available through this website. You can also reserve through phone call during open hours.

4 years old and younger children cannot participate in white water rafting activities for safety reasons.

60 years old and older can participate, but they are required to have more safety equipments.

Once the reservation is confirmed cancelation fee is expected if the reservation is not cancelled at least 3 days before.

For safety training and other purposes, you are expected to arrived at least an hour before.
